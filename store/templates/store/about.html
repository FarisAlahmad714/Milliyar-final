Established in 2021 ,Arabic for 'billion', Milliyar was chosen to represent our brand's effort to bridge the gap between the rich, diverse culture of the East and the vibrant, fast-paced culture of the West.

Our client base is primarily made up of the internationally-minded, the third-culture-individuals, and those who don't want to be constrained by one single aesthetic; at Milliyar, we will always emphasize on multicultural unification because it is the pure embodiment of Milliyar.

A voice for street wear & gear, we offer an array of creative premium clothing wear for different generations of creatives .
We aim to forge collaborations with brands that align with our standards & principles. Formed by love for hip-hop , art , skate , cultural fashion ,Milliyar pursues to spread from the Los Angeles community into a global prodigy .
